3 Craft Ideas

Felt Bow

Felt bows are easy to make. You can make them many ways,there are to main ways to make it: The first way you can make it is with these items, Felt, Hot Glue,and if you want a hair pin. The second way to make it is using Felt, String, and a hair pin if you want.

Paper Snowflake

Paper Snowflakes are one of the easiest crafts that are on this list. The only thing you need is paper. It is not an origami Paper Snowflakes are Super easy to make.

Sqishy

Sqishies are fun and relaxing to make.The only things you need is a few pieces of ripped up toilet paper and regular paper. You can make them with only toilet paper if you want but it is harder.
